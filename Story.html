Brand Introduction

MOLYVIVI is a technology clothing brand originating from Italy. It is also a diversified technology clothing enterprise integrating R&D , production and sales.

MOLYVIVI has an advanced technology team and perfect global industry chain. At the same time, MOLYVIVI is also a leader in technological apparel over the world , taking the lead in the application of functional microcapsules, multi-functional yarns, phase change materials, nano finishing technology in the apparel field.

Brand Concept

Wear the technology

Science and technology have been changing our life. Science and technology make life better. Adhering to the brand concept of "wear the technology ", MOLYVIVI concentrates on the application of new textile technology, using the best products and the most "affordable luxury" for women's clothing worldwide.

Design Concept

Wear the technology

The design concept of MOLYVIVI comes from Milan, the capital of fashion . Girls here prefer to wear leggings to go out. Besides comfort, they can highlight their attractive figure.

The designer of MOLYVIVI combines many design elements of Milan and combines technology with fashion perfectly.
